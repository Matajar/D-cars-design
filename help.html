üìù

Leasing a car is a great option for long-term use. Here's how it works:

  2. Select your preferred vehicle

Frequently Asked Questions

What forms of payment do you accept?
‚ñº
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. For car purchases, we also accept bank transfers and certified checks.
How do I extend my rental period?
‚ñº
You can extend your rental directly through the app by going to "My Rentals" and selecting the "Extend" option. Extensions are subject to availability and must be requested at least 3 hours before the scheduled return time.
What happens if I return the car late?
‚ñº
Late returns are charged at 150% of the hourly rate for the first 3 hours. Beyond 3 hours, a full additional day will be charged. Please contact customer service if you anticipate being late to avoid additional fees.
Is insurance included in my rental?
‚ñº
Basic liability insurance is included in all rentals. Additional coverage options including collision damage waiver (CDW), personal accident insurance (PAI), and roadside assistance are available for purchase during the booking process.
Can I modify my lease terms?
‚ñº
Yes, lease terms can be modified in certain circumstances. Contact our leasing department to discuss options such as extending your lease, adjusting mileage allowance, or early termination. Modification fees may apply.
